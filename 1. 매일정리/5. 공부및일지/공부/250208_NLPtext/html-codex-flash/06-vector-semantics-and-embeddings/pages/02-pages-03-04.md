- source_text: While substitutions between some pairs of words like car / automobile or water / H2O are truth preserving, the words are still not identical in meaning. - source_text: Semantic Frames and Roles Closely related to semantic fields is the idea of a semantic frame. A semantic frame is a set of words that denote perspectives or participants in a particular type of event.
