\hat{Y} = \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{argmax} P(Y|X)
= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{argmax} \frac{1}{Z(X)} exp (\sum_{k=1}^K w_k F_k(X, Y)) (8.27)
= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{argmax} exp (\sum_{k=1}^K w_k \sum_{i=1}^n f_k(y_{i−1}, y_i, X, i)) (8.28)
= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{argmax} \sum_{k=1}^K w_k \sum_{i=1}^n f_k(y_{i−1}, y_i, X, i) (8.29)

Korean Translation
$\hat{Y} = \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{\text{argmax }} P(Y \mid X)$
$= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{\text{argmax }} \frac{1}{Z(X)} \exp \left(\sum_{k=1}^K w_k F_k(X, Y)\right)$ (8.27)
$= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{\text{argmax }} \exp \left(\sum_{k=1}^K w_k \sum_{i=1}^n f_k(y_{i-1}, y_i, X, i)\right)$ (8.28)
$= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{\text{argmax }} \sum_{k=1}^K w_k \sum_{i=1}^n f_k(y_{i-1}, y_i, X, i)$ (8.29)
$= \underset{Y \in \mathcal{Y}}{\text{argmax }} \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{k=1}^K w_k f_k(y_{i-1}, y_i, X, i)$ (8.30)
Study Note

Source Text
Part-of-speech taggers are evaluated by the standard metric of accuracy. Named entity recognizers are evaluated by recall, precision, and F_1 measure. Recall that recall is the ratio of the number of correctly labeled responses to the total that should have been labeled; precision is the ratio of the number of correctly labeled responses to the total labeled; and F-measure is the harmonic mean of the two.
Korean Translation
품사 태거는 표준 지표인 정확도(accuracy)로 평가됩니다. 개체명 인식기는 재현율(recall), 정밀도(precision), 그리고 $F_1$ 측정값으로 평가됩니다. 재현율은 레이블링되어야 할 전체 정답 중 올바르게 레이블링된 응답의 비율이며, 정밀도는 레이블링된 전체 응답 중 올바르게 레이블링된 것의 비율임을 상기하십시오. 그리고 $F$ 측정값은 이 둘의 조화 평균입니다.
Study Note
NER 평가에 재현율과 정밀도를 사용하는 이유를 짐작할 수 있습니다. 대부분의 단어가 개체가 아닌 'O' 태그이기 때문에 정확도는 큰 의미가 없기 때문입니다.

The fact that named entity tagging has a segmentation component which is not present in tasks like text categorization or part-of-speech tagging causes some problems with evaluation. For example, a system that labeled Jane but not Jane Villanueva as a person would cause two errors, a false positive for O and a false negative for I-PER. In addition, using entities as the unit of response but words as the unit of training means that there is a mismatch between the training and test conditions.
Korean Translation
