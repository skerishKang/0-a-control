An alternative approach to doing QA is to query a pretrained language model, forcing a model to answer a question solely from information stored in its parameters. For example Roberts et al. (2020) use the T5 language model, which is an encoder-decoder architecture pretrained to fill in masked spans of text. Fig. 23.16 shows the architecture; the deleted spans are marked by , and the system is trained to have the decoder generating the missing spans (separated by ).
