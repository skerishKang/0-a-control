## Block 9
- type: paragraph
- source_text: In the reading comprehension task, a system is given a question and the passage in which the answer should be found. In the full two-stage QA task, however, systems are not given a passage, but are required to do their own retrieval from some document collection. A common way to create open-domain QA datasets is to modify a reading comprehension dataset. For research purposes this is most commonly done by using QA datasets that annotate Wikipedia (like SQuAD or HotpotQA). For training, the entire (question, passage, answer) triple is used to train the reader.
