Source Text
In this chapter, we explored the concept of dependency parsing, which focus on the direct relations between words in a sentence. We looked at different approaches to dependency parsing, including transition-based, graph-based and neural-based dependency parsing. We also discussed the standard evaluation metrics for dependency parsing, LAS and UAS.

Block 3
list
Source Text
- Dependency: A direct syntactic relation between a head and its dependent.
- Graph-Based Parsing: An approach that finds the highest-scoring tree in a graph of possible dependencies.
- Transition-Based Parsing: An approach that builds a dependency tree using a sequence of shift-reduce like transitions.
- LAS: Labeled Attachment Score, which considers both correct head and correct label.
- UAS: Unlabeled Attachment Score, which considers only the correct head.
Korean Translation
- 의존 관계(Dependency): 지배어(head)와 의존어(dependent) 사이의 직접적인 통사적 관계.
- 그래프 기반 파싱(Graph-Based Parsing): 가능한 의존 관계 그래프에서 가장 점수가 높은 트리를 찾는 접근법.
- 전이 기반 파싱(Transition-Based Parsing): 시프트-리듀스와 유사한 전이 시퀀스를 사용하여 의존 트리를 구축하는 접근법.
- LAS: 레이블된 부착 점수, 올바른 지배어와 올바른 라벨을 모두 고려함.
- UAS: 레이블되지 않은 부착 점수, 올바른 지배어만을 고려함.
