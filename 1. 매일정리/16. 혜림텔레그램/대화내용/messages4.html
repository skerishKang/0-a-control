Jimin's diary

지민의 일지

today I've started with my first log

Filming my first log

​

I came back after a lot of worrying about what to say.

어떤 말을 해야 될지 되게 고민을 많이 하고 왔다

​

When I saw

brother Jin,

진형이나

or brother Rap Monster

랩몬스터형이

uploaded their diaries,

일지를 올린 걸 봤을 때

I used to laugh

되게 옆에서

and mock very much

비웃고,

by their side

웃고 했었는데

but now

막상

that I'm trying to film

내가 찍으려고 하니

I've been worrying a lot

어떤 말부터 꺼내야 될지

about what to say first

되게 고민을 많이 했다

now that I'm here filming

이렇게 와서 찍으니까

It feels really awkward

되게 또 어색한 거 같다

It was posted

on Twitter and blog

yesterday,

어제 트위터랑 블로그에 공개가 되었는데

and as soon as it was posted,

공개가 되자마자

I uploaded a photo

사진을 올리는데

Out of the blue,

생뚱맞게

I asked for a drawing,

그림을 그려 달라는 둥

I said something really unexpected,

되게 생뚱맞은 말을 했는데

but many people

많은 사람들이

drew pictures,

그림을 그려서

and send them, really,

보내줘서 되게

It made me feel good.

기분이 좋았던 것 같다

Now that it's out there I feel like I need to work even harder

공개된만큼 더 열심히 해야 될 것 같다
