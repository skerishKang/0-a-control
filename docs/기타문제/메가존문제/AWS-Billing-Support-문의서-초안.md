## 1. 제목

Request for billing and account usage clarification regarding Padiem AWS account

## 2. 영문 문의 초안

Hello,

We are requesting clarification regarding AWS charges associated with an account related to Padiem Co., Ltd.

Our company understood that the relevant project-based AWS usage had effectively ended around 2021. However, a billing dispute later arose through a third-party billing structure involving Megazone / Cloudgram, and we now need to confirm the actual basis of the charges from AWS records.

We would like to request clarification on the following points:

1. The exact AWS Account ID associated with the disputed billing
2. Which AWS services generated charges after 2021
3. Which regions were involved in those charges
4. Whether AWS KMS-related charges existed and on what basis
5. Whether there were remaining resources that continued generating charges
6. Seller of record for the account
7. Billing contact or account contact information structure
8. Whether there is any account closure, suspension, or termination history

Our purpose is not to make a legal accusation through AWS Support, but to confirm the factual billing structure and usage basis from AWS records.

Thank you.

Padiem Co., Ltd.  
Cheolwon Kang  
Director

## 3. 한글 메모

이 문서는 AWS Billing / Support 문의 시 사용할 초안이다.

핵심은 다음과 같다.

1. 분쟁 호소보다 원장 확인에 집중
2. 실제 과금 서비스와 리전을 특정
3. KMS와 잔존 리소스 여부 확인
4. seller of record 및 billing contact 구조 확인
